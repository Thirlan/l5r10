Base Classes

Bushi

Bushi are the warrior class of the Empire of Rokugan, trained in the arts of combat and honor. They are skilled in both armed and unarmed combat, and are expected to uphold the code of bushi, which emphasizes courage, loyalty, and martial prowess.

Shugenja

Shugenja are the spiritual practitioners of the Empire of Rokugan, trained in the arts of magic and the elements. They are skilled in manipulating the fundamental forces of nature, and are expected to maintain a balance between their spiritual practices and their duties to the Empire.

  • Shugenjas gain 3 spells every time they reach a new school rank.

Courtier

Courtiers are the diplomats and administrators of the Empire of Rokugan, trained in the arts of politics and social interaction. They are skilled in negotiation, intrigue, and maintaining relationships with other clans and the Imperial Court.

Ninja

Ninjas are the stealthy operatives of the Empire of Rokugan, trained in the arts of espionage and subterfuge. They are skilled in infiltration, sabotage, and assassination, and are expected to operate in secrecy while serving their clans or the Imperial Court.

Monk

Monks are wanderers and ascetics who have forsaken worldly possessions and attachments in pursuit of spiritual enlightenment. They often live in remote monasteries or travel the land, spreading their teachings and offering aid to those in need. Monks are known for their discipline, inner strength, and mastery of martial arts, using their bodies as instruments of both peace and combat.
