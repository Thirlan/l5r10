Centipede Clan

An extremely devout and matriarchal people, the Centipede clan devoted themselves to the worship of the Sun for centuries. Their lands are to the east of Treacherous Pass in the Mountains of Regret lies the Centipede valley which was open to the sea, Tani Senshio.

Families

Moshi

Trait: +1 Intelligence

The Moshi family line bore mostly female children, the first Moshi had eleven daughters and no sons, and had become matriarchal because of it.

Schools

Moshi Shugenja

The practices of the Moshi shugenja were developed over the course of many years spent in unwavering devotion to the Sun Goddess. The Moshi are highly adaptable. Their ability to gain an additional Affinity during the day, particularly since it is not accompanied by an additional Deficiency, allows them extreme versatility in their spell-casting choices, enough to rival even the Isawa of the Phoenix Clan.

Keywords: Shugenja

Benefit: +1 Awareness

Skills: Athletics, Calligraphy (Cipher), Lore: Theology, Meditation, Sailing (Navigation), Spellcraft, any one Skill

Honor: 4.5

Outfit: Robes, Wakizashi, Knife, Scroll Satchel, Traveling Pack, 10 koku

Affinity/Deficiency: Air / Earth

Spells: Sense, Commune, Summon, 3 Air and 3 Fire spells

Techniques: Favor of the Sun - No family has been more reverent of the various incarnations of the Sun throughout their history, and their piety has granted them great favor with the Heavens.

  • During the day, you gain an additional Affinity for Fire spells. This benefit is lost at night.
  • You gain a Free Raise on any spell with the Thunder keyword.
